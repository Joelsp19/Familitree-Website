Why use FAMILITREE?

Have you ever had that uncle with a funny accent call, but you can't remember his name? Or a birthday of a distant cousin that you forgot? Or do you just want to connect to your family more? Well, your problems are solved with FAMILITREE, a website that helps you create and organize your family tree. With this tool by your side, no more gaps between your family members.
